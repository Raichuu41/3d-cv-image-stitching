- SURF (Speeded Up Robust Features)
    bad at illumination changes, viewpoint changes
- FAST (Features from Accelerated Segment Test)
    detects too many features
- MSER (Maximally Stable Extremal Regions)
    highest sensitiveness to blurring changes
- Harris corner detector
    de-noising should be done before detecting corners
- Focus on illimunation and viewpoint changes, noise removal and changed due to blurriness
